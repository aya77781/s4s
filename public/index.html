What is $forS?

$forS means "Students for Students."

But it's much deeper than just words — it's a symbolic equation:

$

stands for a former student — someone who has graduated, found stability, and now has money, a job, a network, or power.

→
S

stands for the current student — someone still struggling, still figuring things out, still fighting for their future.

The mission of $forS is to connect the "$" to the "S" —
so that the $ can help the S, until the S becomes a $, and helps the next one.

It's a chain of solidarity, a loop of empowerment.

$forS is a platform designed to improve the academic journey by connecting students with alumni and partner companies. During their studies, students receive free membership, granting them access to resources such as tutoring, job boards, internships, and academic guidance. Upon graduation, those who secure employment contribute $10 a month if their salary exceeds $1000, creating a self-sustaining ecosystem where each new generation benefits from the support of the previous one.

Donors can also join the effort with a $10 monthly pledge that expands the platform's capacity to fund scholarships, interest-free loans, healthcare services, and discounts on various everyday expenses. This collective approach not only addresses financial hurdles but also ensures students have the tools to excel academically, maintain their health, and enjoy a richer student life experience.

Additionally, alumni have continuous access to certain benefits, including networking events, ongoing professional development opportunities, and discounted services. Partner companies contribute by offering exclusive internships, project collaborations, and tailored job openings to the $forS community.

Our Story

The idea of $forS came from a casual conversation with friends. They asked me, "For you what makes a good engineering education?" I thought about it and said, "I believe that when students are in an environment where we really care about them, they do their best." That simple answer sparked something in me.

I realized that education should focus more on students and their needs, but often it doesn't. From that moment, the idea for $forS was born. I wanted to create a platform where students are the priority. At $forS, we provide personalized support, resources, and a learning environment that truly cares about students.

Our goal is to help students succeed by giving them the tools and attention they deserve!

Who Are You?

1

You're a student

For the first five years of your university life we will try to make it perfect for you and don't worry about money, internships or even your student job. Even if you need to pay your scholar fees we're here to help — all this with $0.

2

You're an alumni

You had a great experience with us and now you've found a job — CONGRATULATIONS! Now it's your turn to help. If your salary is more than $1000, you'll pay $10/month for at least 5 years to help other students like we helped you.

3

You're a donor

You were once a student, and you know how hard it can be. You want to help others avoid the same struggle. We are truly grateful for your generosity — THANK YOU FOR YOUR DONATION.

4

You're a partner

You understand that today's students are tomorrow's workforce. By investing in their success, you're not just giving charity — you're building a pipeline of talented, loyal future employees while strengthening your brand's social impact.
